TOPIC: Sequences & Series

ARITHMETIC PROGRESSION (AP):
- General term: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 × (2a + (n-1)d) = n/2 × (a + aₙ)
- a = first term, d = common difference

GEOMETRIC PROGRESSION (GP):
- General term: aₙ = a × r^(n-1)
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = a(rⁿ - 1)/(r - 1) for r ≠ 1
- Sum to infinity: S∞ = a/(1-r) for |r| < 1
- a = first term, r = common ratio

IMPORTANT SUMS:
- Sum of first n naturals: n(n+1)/2
- Sum of squares: n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
- Sum of cubes: [n(n+1)/2]²

HARMONIC PROGRESSION (HP):
- Reciprocals form an AP
- Harmonic Mean of a, b = 2ab/(a+b)

AM-GM-HM INEQUALITY:
AM ≥ GM ≥ HM (for positive numbers)
AM = (a+b)/2, GM = √(ab), HM = 2ab/(a+b)

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Using wrong formula for sum (finite vs infinite GP)
- Forgetting |r| < 1 condition for infinite GP sum
- Off-by-one errors in indexing

SOLUTION TEMPLATE:
1. Identify if AP, GP, or HP
2. Find a (first term) and d or r
3. Apply appropriate formula
4. Check boundary conditions
